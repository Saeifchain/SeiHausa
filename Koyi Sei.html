Menene Sei Network?

SEI wani Layer-1 blockchain ne da aka gina musamman domin samar da saurin gaske ga kasuwancin dijital (trading), DeFi, gaming, AI applications, da sauran manyan aikace-aikacen Web3. Burinsa shi ne ya haɗa saurin tsarin Web2 da tsaron blockchain.

Ma'anonin Farfajiyar Blockchain

Sei Giga

Sei Giga wani sabon babban shiri ne (roadmap/upgrade) na blockchain ɗin Sei wanda aka ƙera domin ƙara saurin aiki da ƙarfin sarrafa ma'amaloli sosai.

SIP-3

SIP-3 shine sabon Sei Improvement Proposal na haɓaka saurin aiki da sarrafa ma'amaloli a kan Sei.

Autobahn

Autobahn shine sabon consensus protocol na Sei Giga wanda aka gina domin kawar da matsalar da ke hana yawancin blockchains sauri: validator guda ɗaya ne kawai ke gina block a lokaci guda.
