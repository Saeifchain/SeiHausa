    Bayanin Autobahn

    Menene Autobahn a fasaha?

    Autobahn shine sabon consensus protocol na Sei Giga wanda aka gina domin kawar da matsalar da ke hana yawancin blockchains sauri: validator guda ɗaya ne kawai ke gina block a lokaci guda. Maimakon haka, Autobahn yana ba da damar validators da yawa su yi aiki lokaci guda. (Sei Blog)

    Don fahimta, bari mu fara da Tendermint.

    Yadda Tendermint ke aiki

    A Tendermint:

    1. Ana zaɓar validator ɗaya (proposer).
    2. Ya tara transactions ya gina block.
    3. Ya aika block ga sauran validators.
    4. Kowa ya yi prevote.
    5. Kowa ya yi precommit.
    6. A tabbatar da block.
    Validator A → Block #100
                              ↓
                          Prevote
                              ↓
                         Precommit
                              ↓
                           Commit

    Matsalar ita ce:

    • Duk network ɗin yana jiran proposer guda ɗaya.
    • Idan proposer ya yi jinkiri, kowa zai jira.
    • Ana haɗa data dissemination, consensus, da execution a lokaci guda.

    Wannan yana zama babban bottleneck yayin da TPS ya ƙaru. (Sei Blog)

    Autobahn: Sabuwar Tsari

    Autobahn ya raba blockchain zuwa manyan sassa uku:

    1. Data Availability Layer (Lanes)
    2. Consensus Layer (Cut of Tips)
    3. Async Execution

    Wannan rabuwa ce ke ba Sei Giga damar kaiwa daruruwan dubban TPS. (docs.sei.io)

    1. Lanes (Hanyoyi)

    Ka yi tunanin Autobahn na Jamus mai hanyoyi da yawa.

    Maimakon hanya ɗaya:

    [Validator A]

    Autobahn yana da hanyoyi da yawa:

    Lane 1 → Validator A
                    Lane 2 → Validator B
                    Lane 3 → Validator C
                    Lane 4 → Validator D

    Kowane validator yana da nasa lane.

    A cikin lane:

    Proposal 1
                       ↓
                    Proposal 2
                       ↓
                    Proposal 3
                       ↓
                    Tip

    Kowane proposal yana ɗauke da:

    • jerin transactions
    • position number
    • link zuwa proposal na baya

    Saboda haka validators da yawa suna yada transactions lokaci guda. (Sei Blog)

    Amfanin Lanes

    Maimakon:

    Validator guda 1
                    ↓
                    1000 tx

    za ka samu:

    Validator A → 1000 tx
                    Validator B → 1000 tx
                    Validator C → 1000 tx
                    Validator D → 1000 tx

    Dukkaninsu suna aiki a lokaci guda. Bandwidth ɗin network gaba ɗaya ake amfani da shi maimakon na mutum guda.

    Proof of Availability (PoA)

    Kafin a yi consensus, dole ne a tabbatar cewa data tana nan.

    Proposal
                       ↓
                    Votes
                       ↓
                    PoA Certificate

    Da zarar proposal ya samu f+1 votes, ana samar da Proof of Availability (PoA). Wannan yana nuna cewa:

    Aƙalla validator ɗaya mai gaskiya yana da cikakken data.

    Saboda haka consensus baya buƙatar sauke duk transaction data kafin ya ci gaba. (Sei Blog)

    2. Cut of Tips (Zuciyar Autobahn)

    Wannan shine abin da mutane da yawa ke wahalar fahimta.

    A Tendermint:

    Consensus on Block

    A Autobahn:

    Consensus on Tips

    Misali:

    Lane A → Tip A
                    Lane B → Tip B
                    Lane C → Tip C
                    Lane D → Tip D

    Leader zai tattara:

    [Tip A, Tip B, Tip C, Tip D]

    Wannan ake kira Cut ko Cut of Tips:

    Cut = [
                     Tip A,
                     Tip B,
                     Tip C,
                     Tip D
                    ]

    Sannan validators su yi consensus akan wannan cut ɗin. (Sei Blog)

    Me Cut yake nufi?

    Tip A = Proposal 10
                    Tip B = Proposal 15
                    Tip C = Proposal 20
                    Tip D = Proposal 7

    to duk proposals da suke ƙasa da waɗannan tips suna shiga ordering ta atomatik.

    Lane A : 1-10
                    Lane B : 1-15
                    Lane C : 1-20
                    Lane D : 1-7

    Ba sai an yi consensus akan proposal ɗaya bayan ɗaya ba. Consensus guda ɗaya yana tabbatar da dubban transactions lokaci guda.

    3. Async Execution

    Wannan shi ne babban sirrin saurin Sei Giga.

    A blockchain na gargajiya:

    Order tx
                    ↓
                    Execute tx
                    ↓
                    Compute State
                    ↓
                    Consensus
                    ↓
                    Finalize

    Idan execution ya yi nauyi:

    Consensus ya tsaya

    A Sei Giga:

    Consensus
                    ↓
                    Finalize

    Execution yana gudana a gefe:

    Block N  → Consensus
                    Block N+1 → Consensus
                    Block N+2 → Consensus

                              ||
                              ||
                              \/

                    Execution of Block N

    Wato consensus baya jiran execution. (docs.sei.io)

    Misali

    Ethereum style:

    Block 100
                    ↓
                    jira execution
                    ↓
                    Block 101

    Autobahn:

    Block 100 finalized
                    Block 101 finalized
                    Block 102 finalized

                    Execution na Block 100 yana gudana a baya

    Saboda haka CPU-intensive smart contracts ba sa rage saurin consensus. (docs.sei.io)

    Bambanci Tsakanin Tendermint da Autobahn

    Abu Tendermint Autobahn
    Proposer Guda ɗaya Da yawa
    Data dissemination Sequential Parallel lanes
    Consensus Akan block Akan cut of tips
    Execution Cikin consensus path Async

    Throughput Ƙasa Mafi girma sosai

    Finality Sauri Sub-second target

    A takaice, Lanes suna ba validators damar yada transactions a lokaci guda, Cut of Tips yana ba network damar yin consensus akan dubban proposals a zagaye guda, sannan Async Execution yana cire execution daga hanyar consensus gaba ɗaya. Wannan haɗin ne ya sa Sei Giga ke da burin kaiwa 200,000+ TPS tare da ƙasa da 400ms finality yayin da yake riƙe tsaron BFT.
