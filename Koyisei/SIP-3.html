Sei Improvement Proposal-3 (SIP-3)

04 Yuni 2026 • Rubutu daga SeiHausa • Sei · EVM · Blockchain

Sei Improvement Proposal-3 (SIP-3) wani babban shirin sauyi ne a cikin tsarin Sei Labs / Sei Network wanda manufarsa ita ce a mayar da Sei ya zama EVM-only blockchain.

Menene SIP-3?

SIP-3 na nufin cire tsarin Cosmos/CosmWasm a hankali, a bar Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM) kaɗai.

A taƙaice

Kafin SIP-3

    Sei yana da tsarin biyu lokaci guda:

  • CosmWasm (Cosmos smart contracts)
  • EVM (Ethereum smart contracts)

Masu gina manhajoji suna iya amfani da ɗaya daga cikin biyun.

Bayan SIP-3

  • Sabbin ayyuka za su fi mayar da hankali kan EVM kawai
  • Za a daina dogaro da CosmWasm
  • Za a rage rikitar tsarin
  • Za a sauƙaƙa kayan aikin developers
  • Za a buɗe hanya zuwa Sei Giga (babban ingantaccen tsarin sauri).

Me ya sa aka kawo SIP-3?

Sei ya bayyana cewa kula da VM biyu yana kawo:

  • ƙarin lambar da ake gyarawa da gwadawa,
  • rikitar wallet,
  • wahalar kula da updates,
  • da rage damar ƙara sauri sosai.
“A mayar da hankali a kan EVM ɗaya domin ƙara performance.”

Waɗanne canje-canje SIP-3 ya kawo?

1. Daina sabbin CosmWasm contracts

An fara dakatar da ƙirƙirar sabbin CosmWasm contracts a mainnet; tsofaffin suna ci gaba a lokacin sauyi.

2. Wallets suna komawa EVM (0x...)

Tsoffin adiresoshin:

  • sei1... → tsarin Cosmos

Sabon tsarin:

  • 0x... → tsarin EVM

Wasu masu wallet suna iya buƙatar haɗa ko matsar da asusu zuwa EVM.

3. IBC assets suna buƙatar kulawa

Wasu kadarori da aka kawo ta IBC (misali USDC.n da wasu bridged assets) suna iya buƙatar migration kafin wasu matakan ƙarshe su fara aiki.

Yaya SIP-3 yake da alaƙa da Sei Giga?

Idan Sei Giga shi ne injin sauri, SIP-3 kamar rage nauyin motar kafin a ƙara gudu.

Manufar ita ce:

  • cire abubuwan da ba dole ba,
  • a rage sarƙaƙiya,
  • sannan a gina babban performance a kan EVM kaɗai.

Shin masu amfani da SEI za su rasa token?

A cewar bayanan migration:

  • SEI token ɗin bai canza ba
  • amma hanyar amfani da wallet da transaction na iya canzawa ga wasu masu amfani.
