Menene Sei Network?

15 Yuni 2026 • Rubutu daga SeiHausa • Sei · Layer-1 · Blockchain

SEI wani Layer-1 blockchain ne da aka gina musamman domin samar da saurin gaske ga kasuwancin dijital, DeFi, gaming, AI applications, da sauran manyan aikace-aikacen Web3.

A sauƙaƙe

Ka yi tunanin:

  • Bitcoin = an gina shi domin adana ƙima da canja kuɗi.
  • Ethereum = an gina shi domin smart contracts.
  • Sei = an gina shi domin aikace-aikacen da ke buƙatar sauri sosai da yawan ma'amaloli.

Abubuwan da suka bambanta Sei

1. Parallel Execution

Maimakon aiwatar da transactions ɗaya bayan ɗaya kamar Ethereum na gargajiya:

TX1 ↓ TX2 ↓ TX3

Sei na iya aiwatar da su lokaci guda:

TX1 TX2 TX3 TX4 │ │ │ │ └────┴────┴────┘ Parallel

Wannan yana ƙara throughput sosai.

2. Saurin Finality

Sei na da finality ƙasa da daƙiƙa guda, wanda ke sa ya dace da trading, gaming da payments.

3. Autobahn Consensus (Sei Giga)

Sabon tsarin Autobahn yana amfani da:

  • Multi-proposer lanes
  • Cut-of-tips consensus
  • Async execution

Domin kaiwa sama da 200,000 TPS tare da finality ƙasa da 400ms.

4. EVM Compatibility

Masu haɓaka software da ke amfani da Ethereum za su iya kawo dApps dinsu zuwa Sei ba tare da sake rubuta komai daga farko ba.

Menene SEI Token?

SEI token yana da manyan amfani guda huɗu:

  1. Biyan transaction fees.
  2. Staking don kare network.
  3. Samun rewards daga staking.
  4. Voting a governance.

Waɗanne Projects suke anfani da Sei?

Ana amfani da Sei wajen:

  • Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs)
  • Perpetual trading platforms
  • NFT marketplaces
  • Blockchain games
  • AI agents
  • Stablecoin da payment systems

Me yasa mutane da yawa suke bibiyar Sei?

Saboda yana ƙoƙarin warware matsalar da yawancin blockchains ke da ita:

Yadda za a samu sauri irin na Web2 ba tare da sadaukar da decentralization ba.

Idan haɓakawar Sei Giga + Autobahn ta cimma abin da aka tsara, Sei zai iya zama ɗaya daga cikin manyan blockchain infrastructures da za su iya ɗaukar miliyoyin masu amfani lokaci guda.

A matsayinka na mai sha'awar crypto, abu mafi muhimmanci da za ka fahimta shi ne cewa Sei ba kawai coin ba ne; cikakken blockchain ecosystem ne, yayin da SEI shi ne token ɗin da ke aiki a cikin wannan ecosystem.
